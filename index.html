Hey there, I'm Zuzka

I used to think I wasn't creative enough to make anything with my hands. For years, I used them mostly for typing on a keyboard and clicking the mouse.

Then I learned to knit. Later, crochet. And something shifted.

Crochet and knitting became my way of slowing down after busy workdays. What started as a personal hobby slowly turned into something I began sharing with others.

I'm self-taught, which means I still clearly remember what it feels like to be a beginner.
The confusion. The tight stitches. The "Am I doing this right?" moments.

You don't need to be creative to learn crochet.
You don't need to be perfect from the start.
You just need to be curious.

I'll take care of the rest.
